Strategic Partnerships

Collaborating with global and local entities to drive digital change, policy influence, and civic innovation across Ethiopia.

National Electoral Board of Ethiopia (NEBE)

Licensed to undertake online voter education for both the Six and Seventh Ethiopian General election. Licensed for election observation focusing on political parties' social media activities.

Center for International Private Enterprise (CIPE)

Partner for civic entrepreneurship incubation. Past recipient of organizational strengthening fund for 'Restoring Ethiopia’s Civic Space'.

Agence française de développement (AFD)

Implementing 'Ethiopian Civil Society Engagement Program'. Accelerating Digital Activism and AI skills among civic space actors.

National Democratic Institute (NDI)

Partnered to enhance capacity of marginalized Youth in National Dialogue processes. Pioneered AI-assisted 'Mekeker-Ethiopia' digital campaign.

UN Capital Development Fund & Women’s World Banking

Advocating for women's digital financial inclusion. Developed Women-Centered Design Principles for Mobile Money Service Providers.

META

META

Active partnership to advance digital rights promotion, privacy, and responsible technology use within Ethiopia's digital landscape.

Australian Embassy

Implemented online voter education project targeting people with disabilities, first time and women voters, reaching over 400,000 Ethiopians using Amharic and Afan Oromo.

Grand Duchy of Luxembourg Embassy

Promoting Digital Safety education and practice among Ethiopian Civic Space actors to foster a safer digital environment.

Coalition of Civil Societies for Election (CECOE)

Member organization utilizing digital expertise to advance election dynamics through locally nurtured innovation.

Ethiopian CSO Council (ECSOC)

Active MoU aimed at strengthening capacity and interconnectedness of CSOs through platforms developed by DRSE.G.

Civic Tech Innovation Network (CTIN)

Mapped from Ethiopia for employing civic technology solutions. Contributor to case studies and online discussion forums.

Alliance of CSOs of Tigray (ACSOT)

Digitally empowering ACSOT member organizations through DERSHA.net and TSEDAL platforms for networking and capacity building.
